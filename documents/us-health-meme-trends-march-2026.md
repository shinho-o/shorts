## 🎨 Visual Style Guide

### **What's Working in March 2026**

**DO:**
- Oversaturated colors (throwback to 2016 trend)
- Fast cuts (under 15 seconds ideal)
- Text overlays (large, bold, easy to read)
- Authentic reactions (not overly polished)
- Behind-the-scenes tech (sensors, dashboards)

**DON'T:**
- Overly corporate/salesy tone
- Long intros (hook in first 2 seconds)
- Poor lighting (invest in ring light)
- Silence (always use trending audio)
